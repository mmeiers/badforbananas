About

about the banana man

No, I don’t really know where the “Bad for Bananas” came from, but I think it’s important to add a touch of humor to the things that interest you. All my life I’ve been the creative type: drawing and painting from an early age. As I went through school, I just kept going with the creative mode. I specialize in digital and web design, though I’m experienced in print as well. I hope to continue employing this enjoyment in design in all areas of my work. And if you can’t tell, I really DO like bananas!
